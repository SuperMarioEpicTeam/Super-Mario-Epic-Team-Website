Intro:

Super Mario Epic Wii is an ambitious mod of New Super Mario Bros. Wii, currently in development. Our project aims for an intense and creative experience, with advanced mechanics and a unique artistic style. Designed by a team of long-time fans of the NSMBWii game, we offer our future players a well-crafted experience that forces them to enhance their gameplay and feel comfortable in the game. The mod has been in development since August 26, 2023, and the release date will be TBD. Inspired by the mechanics of very diverse Kaizo games such as the Grand Poo World series, Casio Mario World, Lydian Mario World, and much more, this game offers an epic adventure through a storyline outside the Mario universe.

Note: The content of this mod is currently private and only accessible to the development team, which you can join :).

This mod features:
  • +120 new levels, divided over 12 worlds
  • High Mechanism Difficulties inspired of many Kaizo games.
  • Personalized environments and graphics.
  • Custom power-ups
  • And much more...
